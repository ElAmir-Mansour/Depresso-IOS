## 🎯 Feature Prioritization Framework

### How to Decide What to Build

For each feature, score on:

#### 1. User Impact (1-10)
- How many users affected?
- How much does it improve their experience?

#### 2. Business Impact (1-10)
- Retention improvement?
- Acquisition improvement?
- Monetization potential?

#### 3. Effort (1-10, inverse)
- Engineering hours needed?
- Complexity/risk?

#### 4. Feasibility (1-10)
- Technical constraints?
- External dependencies?

**Priority Score = (User Impact + Business Impact + Effort + Feasibility) / 4**

## 🎓 Learning from Analysis

### Key Takeaways for Any iOS Medical App:

1. **Auth Must Be Frictionless** - Every screen before sign-in loses 20-30% of users
2. **Notifications Are Not Optional** - They're the #1 retention driver
3. **Empty States Are Critical** - First impression determines engagement
4. **Medical ≠ Clinical** - Can be warm and professional simultaneously
5. **Trust Takes Time** - Progressive disclosure, not overwhelming
6. **Data Must Tell Stories** - Raw metrics are meaningless to users
7. **Accessibility = Larger Audience** - 15% of population has disabilities
8. **Widget = Free Marketing** - Visible on home screen = constant reminder
9. **Haptics Matter** - Especially for emotional validation
10. **Privacy Badges Build Trust** - Show, don't just say
