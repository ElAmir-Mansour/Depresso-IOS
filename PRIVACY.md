# Privacy Policy for Depresso

**Effective Date:** March 22, 2026

At Depresso, we are committed to protecting your privacy while providing you with an advanced, AI-driven journaling experience. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information, particularly regarding health metrics, motion data, and AI-driven analysis.

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Health Data (Apple HealthKit)
Depresso integrates with Apple HealthKit to provide holistic wellness insights. 
- **Data Points:** We may request access to read your sleep patterns, step counts, and heart rate metrics.
- **Purpose:** This data is used solely to correlate your physical activity with your emotional state in the Depresso "Insights" dashboard.
- **Control:** You can grant or revoke access to HealthKit data at any time through the iOS "Health" app settings.

### B. Behavioral Metadata (Motion & Typing)
To enhance the accuracy of our emotional analysis, we collect subtle behavioral metadata during your active journaling sessions.
- **Motion Data:** We sample device acceleration to detect physical agitation or restlessness.
- **Typing Cadence:** We measure typing speed and edit frequency to understand your cognitive flow.
- **Purpose:** This data is analyzed by our AI to provide more personalized Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) feedback.

### C. Journal Content (NLP & AI)
The text you write or dictate in your journal is the core of your experience.
- **AI Analysis:** Your entries are processed using Natural Language Processing (NLP) to identify sentiment, emotions, and cognitive distortions.
- **Safety Monitoring:** Our AI monitors content for high-risk indicators (self-harm or severe distress) to provide immediate links to crisis support resources.

### D. Identity & Authentication
- **Guest Mode:** You may use Depresso as a Guest. In this mode, your data is stored primarily on your device.
- **Sign in with Apple:** If you choose to sign in, we receive a unique identifier from Apple. We do not receive your private email address unless you explicitly choose to share it via Apple's secure interface.

## 2. How We Use Your Data
- **Personalization:** To provide tailored mental health insights and CBT reframing.
- **Research:** Anonymized, de-identified behavioral metadata may be used to improve our AI models and contribute to mental health research.
- **App Functionality:** To sync your data across your devices if you are signed in.

## 3. Data Security & Retention
- **Encryption:** All data transmitted between the app and our secure backend (hosted on Vercel/Postgres) is encrypted using industry-standard TLS.
- **Minimal Retention:** We retain your journal entries and analysis only as long as necessary to provide the service. You may delete your account or specific entries at any time.

## 4. Disclosure of Information
- **No Third-Party Sales:** We **never** sell your personal or health data to third parties or advertisers.
- **Emergency Situations:** If our AI detects a severe risk to your safety, we provide you with direct resources (such as the 988 Lifeline) but do not automatically contact authorities unless required by law.

## 5. Your Rights
You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data at any time. For users in "Guest Mode," deleting the app will remove all locally stored data.
